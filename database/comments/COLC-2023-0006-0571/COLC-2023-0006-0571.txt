Generative AI time and time again has been used to aggregate people&#39;s works without their permission for personal or monetary gains. Unlike something like a transformative work on an intellectual property, there is no human process behind creating something with generative AI. It can even be dangerous for businesses. For example, someone could type private information into a generative AI tool and essentially leak all of that information if the tool is public.
