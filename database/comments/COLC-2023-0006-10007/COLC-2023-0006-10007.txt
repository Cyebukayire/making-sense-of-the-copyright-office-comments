Artists have historically had the right to govern who uses their works, or be compensated for use, reproduction, or derivatives of their work.<br/><br/>Artists should have the right to consent and compensation to allow companies to literally make copies of their works to feed into AI, as well as the derivatives that generative AI produces using their works.<br/><br/>AI training does NOT fall under any current definition of Fair Use.<br/><br/>Creative people have a hard enough time without insanely wealthy tech firms vacuuming up their art and feeding it to a generative AI in order to make the tech company wealthy while refusing to give even a penny to the artists who made the AI possible.
