Letting AI image generation companies use copyrighted images with no recourse defeats the purpose of having a copyright in the first place. Not only does this make it hard for artists to make a living, it will have broad implications to American culture as a whole. Society depends on their authors, artists, writers, musicians, etc. in a way that you can not quantify with hard facts or financial figures. Please consider this and consider helping all artists big and small through these difficult times.
