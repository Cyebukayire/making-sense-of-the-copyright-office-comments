Require transparency and licensing of training data. It is unacceptable for AI models to fall under less scrutiny than any other use of copyrighted data based on current arguments claiming they are somehow different. It is ultimately a piece of commercial software that operates entirely on ingested data. That data should be compensated for an properly licensed as any other use of copyrighted content would, especially since the AI models aim to compete and replace those who created the training data within their own markets.
