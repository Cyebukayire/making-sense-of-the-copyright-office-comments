Adding a voice to the need for AI regulation and protections for citizens within governmental language and law.  The opportunities of AI to aid in every day life are high, but the risks to job security and basic work rights are significantly higher and represent an existential threat to many careers and industries which could heavily impact the economy and well being of the nature nation.  As individuals and as a country we seek governmental protection against AI replacing human workers and corporations exploring AI as a means to replace employees.
