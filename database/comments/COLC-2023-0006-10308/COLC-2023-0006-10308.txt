In regards to question number 15, there is a proposal by Margaret Mitchell et al. for a means by which to document and make transparent what data is being used to train A.I. I think this method (the &quot;model cards&quot;) are a good idea, and it should be required for companies that create A.I. tools to have those cards and make them publicly available to anyone who wants to see what an A.I. has been trained on.
