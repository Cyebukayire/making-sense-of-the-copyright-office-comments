The use of AI to impersonate, recreate, or otherwise provide commercial literary content to the public poses an existential threat to the arts. Storytelling is the domain of humanity, and it is our sacred honor to uphold the tradition of passing down stories through the generations. Allowing AI to tell our stories not only robs us of the heart of the human experience, it also threatens the livelihoods of writers everywhere. The financial losses for the writers and publishing houses will be crushing, as the software developers will reap the full monopoly on a multi-billion dollar industry.
