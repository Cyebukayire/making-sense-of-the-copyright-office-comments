Please protect the intellectual rights and human labor required to produce copyrighted material. Do NOT permit AI generated content to be copyrighted. If a work is produced by AI and later edited or amended by human labor, ONLY the portions of that work that are human-created should be eligible for copyright protection. <br/>It is essential that our government do everything in its power to protect the rights of human creativity and innovation. <br/>If we elevate works produced using machine learning to the same status as novel ideas produced by human thought, not only will we immediately and irreparably harm to the labor market for creative professionals, we will also as a society suffer from stagnation and a lack of innovation as the market favors the cheap and fast to produce output of &quot;generative AI&quot; over human innovation.
