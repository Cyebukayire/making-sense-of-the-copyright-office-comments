Content generated by AI should not be eligible for copyright and should be labeled as AI-generated. AI-generated content should be linked to the user with the ability to search the user&rsquo;s prompt history. Copyright holders of original works should be able to look up how their original works are being scanned, referenced, and used by generative AI platforms. AI-generated content that is distributed for profit (e.g. entertainment including but not limited to literature, art, and film; advertisements etc)  should have to pay royalties to the copyright holders of the original works used as templates and/or references by the AI platform
