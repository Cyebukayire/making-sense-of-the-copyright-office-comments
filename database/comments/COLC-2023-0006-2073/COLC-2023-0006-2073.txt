AI should not be trained off of copyrighted material (at least without permission from the copyright holder) and additionally should not be allowed to be copyrighted. Copyright is a protection of creators rights to their work, AI is inherently meant to imitate or recreate the content from which it&rsquo;s trained on. In the usage of AI, anything it creates is stealing from others in a way a human gaining inspiration would not as humans maintain emotional intelligence which AI do not. AI should be kept away from artists spaces and should not be legally allotted the same rights as an artists who contributes physical, emotional and spiritual effort into creating something and copyrighting it to protect their work.
