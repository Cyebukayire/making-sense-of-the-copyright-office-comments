Work produced using an AI with no to minimal human alteration of the product should not be copyrightable, and to copyright should be at least 75% original writing from a human, since otherwise the author(s) would not have produced a substantial portion of the work. Using copyrighted materials for training an AI should not be an issue if work from an AI alone is not copyrightable, and a human altering that work should be able to make it comply with existing copyright law.
