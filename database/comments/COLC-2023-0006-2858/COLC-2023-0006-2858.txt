AI is a tool, but by itself should not be allowed to product copyrightable works. There are numerous questions about the ownership of the content which goes into training such AI services, and as such all output should not be considered to be owned or copyrighted by the entity using it.<br/><br/>Further, should AI be used to product art, stories, artifacts, or other media or code, anyone who&#39;s likeness, content including videos, images, art, stories, code or other media artifacts which were used to train the AI should be considered as contributing to the final product, and as such both require their consent for the content produced as well as be fairly compensated.
