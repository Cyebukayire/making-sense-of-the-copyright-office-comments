Artificial intelligence poses an absolute grave threat to the livelihood of the creative workers of America that, in no uncertain terms, provide the SOUL of American culture. Creative works that rely upon the work of actors, writers, visual effects artists, concept artists, animators, and more CANNOT be allowed to simply rip their efforts out from their hands and parrot them repeatedly through AI training sets for the sake of minimization of corporate costs for the sake of maximization of profit.
