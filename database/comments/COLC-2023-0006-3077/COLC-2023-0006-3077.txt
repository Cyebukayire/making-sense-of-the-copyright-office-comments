AI work is inherently derivative and cannot be copyrighted. And it shouldn&#39;t be able to be copyrighted. In the industry we are already seeing a huge influx of spam books churned out in an afternoon: Nonfiction books with poor quality information (Sometimes dangerous information such as the AI generated books in mushroom identification or that contain safety precautions that miss essential steps) and flooding the fiction markets with copycats of famous authors and books. By not allowing people to copyright AI generated works, it protects the writers whose works were used, without permissions or compensation, to create this model, and the income and intellectual property of writers of the future.
