The use of Artificial Intelligence for large industries such as film, games, etc. can be harmful when they replace humans. Artists, voice actors, VFXs, and more rely on these industries to make a living, and having AI being used more commonly makes it especially difficult to get and keep a job when the mindset becomes more &ldquo;I can replace you with a machine.&rdquo; Please look into ways on how to restrict the use of AI when they harm human workers, especially the creatives.
