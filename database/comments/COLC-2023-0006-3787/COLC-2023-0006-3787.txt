AI tech appears to be harmful both to small artists whose work is being recycled by content creation algorithms to mass produce art in their unique style or directly copying parts of their preexisting pieces, and similarly as well to those employed at larger studios and brands who are increasingly at threat of losing their jobs to a machine. AI does not ethically collect the content its models are based on, and the American workforce is bearing the brunt of the consequences for this new, unregulated technology.
