AI content generation technologies should not be trained on anything that was obtained without proper consent from its owner, whether that be data sets, pieces of art, written work, or even one&rsquo;s physical likeness. Being posted to the internet should not be implied consent to be used to train an AI generation model. AI developers should be required to only use content that has been labeled as allow for use in content generation AI for the training of any model where the content generated will be published in any form. <br/>Additionally, content generated by AI should be labeled when published. <br/>AI programs can enjoy the same copyright and patent protection as other pieces of software. Works generated by AI should only be copyrightable to the person who programmed the AI, as long as they do not contain the likeness of another person or content that is already copyright protected. Any works generated by open-source AI programs should not be copyrightable.
