AI and machine learning models must not be allowed to scrape copyrighted intellectual property to create derivative works. The art, voice, or writing of a person must remain their own property, for use only by the creator and those to whom the creator gives express permission. AI models unlawfully use the creative works of hard working citizens and actively harm our ability to make a living through our own creative endeavors. Any content created by an AI must be created only on works that have been allowed by the creator, and any financial benefit or royalties must be paid to the original creator whose work was referenced or scraped by the AI model.
