I am an artist and designer based in VA. In the age of technology, creatives like me have no real choice but to share our work online, whether to market ourselves to attract clients, as part of a day job designing social media graphics for others, or any number of other reasons. In its current iteration, AI scraping is indiscriminate&mdash;it takes visual works from the internet without checking for copyright or usage permissions. It is unethical and unacceptable for the people behind machine learning software to let their projects gather and use massive databases made almost entirely of work painstakingly created by people who did not consent to its use.
