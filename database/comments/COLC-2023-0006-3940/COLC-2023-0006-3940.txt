It is my belief that any work of AI should not be viable for copyright under a human beings name as it was not made by a human being. Similarly, it should not be copyrighted to a company either as no employee of a hypothetical company created the intellectual property in question. Additionally, as a separate issue, all open source engines pull their data from images and content that they do not own the copyright nor have the paid the rights to use, and therefore should be considered copyright infringement upon any firm or individual whose intellectual property was used in the The AI
