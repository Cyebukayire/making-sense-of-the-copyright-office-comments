As long as Artificial Intelligence continue to be trained on datasets containing copyrighted works and/or works taken without the consent of its creators, the content it creates shouldn&#39;t be used at all. Whether it be private individuals or corporations, as long as there is no consent of the creators its data is trained on, AI Generated content cannot be made and used ethically. Regarding its legal status, as typical AI Generated content continues to be made using copyrighted material, it cannot and should not have copyright.
