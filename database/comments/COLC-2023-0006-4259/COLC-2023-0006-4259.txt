AI models that are used for commercial purposes (tv, movies, music) and trained on a particular individual for their visual appearance or style or that are trained to sound like a particular person&#39;s voice should pay out royalty dividends the same as using someone&#39;s voice or image otherwise. Media containing ai generated content should not be able to be mistaken for content which is not ai generated, the same as adult content warnings before films and TV or on albums. AI models that are trained using public data such as those scraped from social media or the internet at large should not be able to be used in commercial purposes
