The U.S. Constitution establishes copyright to &quot;promote the Progress of Science and useful Arts&quot; A prime example of such advancements in the here and now is AI models. Consequently:<br/>Current copyright regulations shouldn&#39;t be interpreted in a manner that complicates the training of these models, as it would contradict the original intent.<br/><br/>The individual training the AI model should have the right to copyright it, akin to the rights granted to software developers over their programs.<br/><br/>The results produced by AI models should be eligible for copyright by the individual using the model, similar to other forms of creative expression, as long as there&#39;s no infringement on existing copyrights.<br/><br/>
