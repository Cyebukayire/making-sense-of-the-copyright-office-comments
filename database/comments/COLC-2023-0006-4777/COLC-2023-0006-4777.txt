Companies that create AIs should be required to license any and all data they use to &quot;train&quot; an AI, unless in the public domain. Works created by AI should not be able to be copyrighted unless it is proven that they have not been trained with or have in their data set, any materials held in copyright that were not license for that use. Any company that sells products created by AI should be required to disclose that information, especially informational and non-fiction media. AI also should be prohibited for use by any government agencies as many AI works have been proven to contain race, gender, and other biases, or have dangerously incorrect information.<br/>Social media and other website should be required to let their users opt-out of having their content and data be sold or licensed for the purpose of training an AI.<br/>AI chat bots should be required to disclose they are AI and not pose as humans.
