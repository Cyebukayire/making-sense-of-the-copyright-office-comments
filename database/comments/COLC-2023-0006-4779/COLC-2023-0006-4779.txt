In my opinion, no new regulations are needed, the current copyright law is sufficient. Plagiarism should be punished regardless of whether AI was used or not. AI tools should be treated the same as any other artistic technique. Artificial intelligence should not be stigmatized, also by the obligation to report its participation in a work - this could cause such work to be valued lower and paid less. Current attempts to create special regulations seem to me to be a struggle for the development of a new market by big players, ultimately unfavorable both for the artist and the user
