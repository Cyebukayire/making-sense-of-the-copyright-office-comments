Content produced by artificial intelligence ought to both compensate the original creators of any material that is still under copyright and clearly cite all original, human generated material referenced, paraphrased, or used in any way to produce the AI generated materials. If an AI was trained on copyright material, then all of those creators deserve compensation, citation, and recognition of their contributions. Furthermore, any historical material that is out of copyright ought to be clearly cited and credited as having been both part of the training and part of the product of AI systems, products, and services.
