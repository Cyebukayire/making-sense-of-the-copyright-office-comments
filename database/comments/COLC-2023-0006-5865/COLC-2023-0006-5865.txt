AI generated content must not, under any circumstances, be granted the protection of copyright.  Copyright may only be extended to humans and their work.  So while the software that produces the content may be protected, the output of the software cannot.<br/><br/>In addition, makers of AI software must not be allowed to use content, of any type, whether protected by copyright or not, in producing their software.  Such content must be purchased for the specific use in producing AI software.  Such use cannot be a condition of an unrelated activity, i.e. user content cannot be used for AI software as a condition of using a social media platform.<br/><br/>The federal government must move quickly to halt the theft and misuse of intellectual property by AI organizations.
