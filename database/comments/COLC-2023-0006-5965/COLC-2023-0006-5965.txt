The use of artificial intelligence to create any creative work should not result in a work that can be copyrighted, as the models used to create these works trawl through the Internet grabbing any relevant data they can, regardless of consent to do so. I am certain that this reality will never change in the foreseeable future in a manner sufficient enough to alter my stance on this matter, as these models continue to be used without consideration for this glaring issue that is fundamentally tied to the function of these generative software.
