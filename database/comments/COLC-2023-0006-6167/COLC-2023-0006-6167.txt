The AI-Generatives base their operation on huge datasets of works that have been taken without any authorization. Many illustrators have seen their works plagiarized in bulk. Voice actors have seen how their own voices, their work tools, are being used without authorization. The same goes for writers, photographers, etc.<br/>And not only that, AI-GEN users, colloquially known as Prompter, have little control over what the AI-Gen produces, according to their own comments, mentioning that they need hundreds of iterations to get the image they need. For this reason, AI-Gen cannot be considered a valid tool, since they do not have control over the process.<br/>In addition, and finally, AI-Gen are seriously precarious the work of workers in the creative industries, who see not only their livelihood at risk, but also their own passion.
