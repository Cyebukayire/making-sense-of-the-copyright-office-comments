Artificial Intelligence is an existential threat to all types of creative work. Corporations are laundering data through nonprofits and educational institutions at the expense of individual artists who do not have the resources to fight back and protect their assets. These companies are raising billions of dollars to supplant us.<br/><br/>I will have no financial incentive to take classes, hone my craft, buy supplies, or spend years improving my work if it can be stolen and distributed without recourse. My work is not only a product. It is a process that is mental, physical, and spiritual, grounded in a specific moment and my life, circumstances, and culture.<br/><br/>A machine cannot be inspired. It calculates. It may be a fun toy for those who do not wish to put in the years of work, but it is basing the output by stealing from thousands and perhaps millions of people. It is wholesale theft and a violation.
