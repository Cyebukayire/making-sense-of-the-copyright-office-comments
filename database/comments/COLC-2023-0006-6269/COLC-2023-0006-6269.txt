To put it simply, the usage of AI generated art is similar to asking someone else to cut out pictures from a magazine and gluing them onto a blank piece of paper. The person generating images takes on the role of an art requester; a person who does not create any actual art themselves. And the AI takes on the role of the compositor: they do not create art themselves but they take other existing art pieces and put them together. The copyright should go to the initial creator of the content used for training. And that content should be opt in, not opt out.
