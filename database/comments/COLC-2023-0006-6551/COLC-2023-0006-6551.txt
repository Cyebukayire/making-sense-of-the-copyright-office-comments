If someone (whether an individual or some form of organization) creates something, it belongs to them; at is, unless they specifically grant it a Creative Commons license or someone asks them for permission to use it.<br/><br/>This should include anything that has been posted on social media. The vendor of the social media platform (whether or not mentioned in their terms of use, which no one reads) should not have permission to feed that to an artificial intelligence algorithm of any kind (whether made by the same vendor or someone else)<br/><br/>If a vendor of a social media platform or artificial intelligence algorithm wants to use it, they should be required to reach out to who created the work.
