AI generated content is already proving to be wildly harmful. Between spreading blatant misinformation, using the likeness of others without consent, and stealing man-made content (as either source material or learning tools), it is not a system that can continue to exist without heavy regulations in place that at the very least require people to OPT IN to having their property/data/likeness used in AI development, as well as making it abundantly clear upon first glance when a piece of content has been AI generated or altered in any way.
