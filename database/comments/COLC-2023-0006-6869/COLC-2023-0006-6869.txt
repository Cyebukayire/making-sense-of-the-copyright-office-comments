Due to the required use of outside material, artwork, writing, etc..., mostly without compensation to creators, AI generated content should not be eligible for copyright. The ability to outright copy artist works and distinct styles creates a dangerous precedent against artists and writers livelihoods. Unless AI companies can guarantee the copyright material is not used in training AI algorithms, which currently no AI has done, then fully AI content should actually violate copyright laws. AI generated content should not be eligible for copyright.
