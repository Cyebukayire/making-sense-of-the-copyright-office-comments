Image generation relies on the stolen intellectual property of countless human beings, many of whom are struggling to make ends meet in an increasingly hostile world. Image generation is an existential threat to the livelihoods of professional artists and craftspeople in many industries, including the entertainment industry. This tech must be regulated heavily--data sets should be ethically sourced and paid for, and AI should not be permitted to displace working artists. Studios already make record profits year over year and this would only allow them to pay artists less.
