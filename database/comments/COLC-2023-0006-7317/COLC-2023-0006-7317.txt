As a writer, the idea of having my intellectual copy stolen has always been a concern. With AI, writers, musicians, actors, artists, and many others fear for the art we create. Knowing that AI can adopt my likeness and simply replicate a work with none of the human intention and emotion behind it is terrifying. AI will destroy art as we know it and warp the human perspective on all creativity. Allowing AI to exist in its current form and progress will only have a negative impact on all areas of creative expression. AI is a violation of inherent privacy which should be given to all humans.
