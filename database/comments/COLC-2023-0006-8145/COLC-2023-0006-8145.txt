AI generated content could infringe on existing copyrights, as some of these technologies are trained using materials without consent from the creators/copyright holders. Unless the technology is trained from materials with consent from copyright holders, AI generated content should not be copyrighted and not be permitted for commercial use. <br/><br/>There should be clear guidelines in place for the use of AI generated content. The lack of regulations can heavily impact the livelihoods of artists, writers, and the creative industry as a whole.
