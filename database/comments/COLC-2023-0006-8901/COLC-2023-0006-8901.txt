AI regulation must contain measures to protect creators not only in the future, but the present as well as the past. Current AI datasets are built off the hard work and creativity of artists all around the world and utilizing said work against them to diminish and destroy their work opportunities and livelihood. We do not ask that AI does not exist or that it even should not exist in the arts.
