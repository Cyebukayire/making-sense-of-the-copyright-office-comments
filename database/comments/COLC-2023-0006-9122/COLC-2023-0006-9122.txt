AI image and audio generation uses preexisting content, made by both multi-billion dollar corporations and starving artists, without consent or loyalties given to the copyright owners. When it comes to humans, this alone would break copyright laws. AI has already been used to scam fans from the biggest media franchises to indie comic artists by generating inauthentic content that pretends to be authentic in order to spread misinformation or commit fraud. Speaking of misinformation, AI generation has also been widely used in generating fake photos of major politicians, journalists, celebrities, billionaires, and more in order to spread misinformation that skews the uninformed public to their agenda. Regulations on AI generation is not only important for the entertainment and arts industries but also essential in the fight against the current biggest problem with our polarized political climate, misinformation and propaganda.
