I believe it should be considered a violation of copyright to use the copyrighted works of another person or entity, without express written consent by said person or entity, in the creation, training, and output of AI models, of any form (photographs, artworks, writing, music, etc.). If one who has created an AI model has obtained the permission of the owner of a copyrighted work to use said work in the training of an AI model, the output of the model should be required to have a copyright disclaimer, unless the necessity of this disclaimer is waived by the owner of the work.
