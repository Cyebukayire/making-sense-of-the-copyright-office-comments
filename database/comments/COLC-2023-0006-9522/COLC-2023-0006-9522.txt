Companies and people should not be able to use copyrighted material belonging to others to train an AI without permission from and, when requested, compensation to the copyright holders. What&#39;s the point of producing intellectual property such as a text or image if it can be stolen by AI producers without recompense? The point of copyright law is to encourage the production of intellectual property. Allowing this intellectual property to be stolen will have a chilling effect on writers, artists, etc.
