Generative AI poses an existential threat to the livelihoods of untold numbers of artists, writers, designers, and other workers in a variety of fields. Most alarmingly, what constitutes the majority of GenAI is simply well-marketed theft, advanced scraping algorithms and programs designed to steal and re-purpose the hard work of others. Government regulation of generative AI is crucial, not just to protecting the valuable output of American workers, but to encouraging innovation and a desire to create in future Americans.
